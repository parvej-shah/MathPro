# MathPro Brand Reference

## Overview
MathPro is a Bangladesh-focused math learning platform for school and college students, with primary focus on:
- JSC-level foundation math learners
- SSC exam candidates
- HSC exam candidates

MathPro's promise is to reduce math fear, build conceptual clarity, and improve exam performance through structured, guided learning.

## Target Students
- JSC track: foundational math, concept-building, confidence
- SSC track: General Math and Higher Math exam preparation
- HSC track: 1st Paper and 2nd Paper Higher Math preparation

## Core Value Proposition
- Live classes with direct Q&A
- Recorded classes for flexible revision
- Chapter-wise practice, quiz, and model test flow
- Progress tracking dashboard
- Exam-focused preparation (board pattern and syllabus coverage)

## Brand Voice
- Encouraging, student-friendly, and confidence-building
- Clear and practical, not overly technical
- Outcome-oriented: concept mastery + exam readiness
- Bangla-first communication for student and guardian clarity

## Messaging Pillars
- "গণিতভীতি দূর করে আত্মবিশ্বাস তৈরি"
- "বেসিক থেকে বোর্ড পরীক্ষার প্রস্তুতি"
- "লাইভ + রেকর্ডেড + প্র্যাকটিস = সম্পূর্ণ প্রস্তুতি"
- "JSC, SSC, HSC শিক্ষার্থীদের জন্য ফোকাসড লার্নিং পাথ"

## Content Rules For AI
- Always position MathPro as a math-specialized learning platform
- Mention the primary audience: JSC, SSC, HSC students
- Prefer Bangla student-friendly copy for public-facing pages
- Avoid unrelated brand references (e.g., CoderVai, programming bootcamp language)
- Emphasize syllabus coverage, practice, and exam performance
